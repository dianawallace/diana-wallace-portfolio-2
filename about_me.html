About Me

My name is Diana Wallace and I am an Illustrator and Web Developer currently based in Denver, Colorado.

I am a dedicated, enthusiastic, and highly motivated illustrator and web developer with experience building websites and creating illustrations. Formally educated in fine art and illustration I have spent over seven years with the Adobe suite of creative applications. I have a passion for art and design and enjoy blending these two mediums with modern web technologies.

This site was built with Cloud9 ide and bootstrap, which is an open source Javascript framework. The source code is available on github.
